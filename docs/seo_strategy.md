# SEO Strategy

## Strategic Positioning

The product competes as a fast, mobile-first discovery engine for Telegram communities and bots. The advantage should come from structured inventory, better mobile UX, cleaner entity pages, and rapid category coverage without sacrificing quality or compliance.

## Operating Bets

- Mobile-first directory pages can outperform slower, cluttered competitors.
- Programmatic SEO works only if pages have real inventory and unique value.
- AI bot and companion categories can support affiliate monetization.
- Owner submissions improve data quality and reduce dependency on scraping.
- Technical SEO and internal links matter more than traditional backlinks in the early phase.

## Guardrails

- Do not index illegal, abusive, exploitative, or non-consensual content.
- Do not bypass source access controls.
- Do not publish thin pages at scale.
- Do not hide sponsorships or affiliate relationships.
- Do not let NSFW growth compromise platform safety.

## Quarterly Objectives

- Q1: Launch schema, seed inventory, core routes, and sitemap system.
- Q2: Expand category coverage and owner submission flow.
- Q3: Add conversion analytics, VIP placements, and affiliate experiments.
- Q4: Build trend reports, partner distribution, and automated quality scoring.
