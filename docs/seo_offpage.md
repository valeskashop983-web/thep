# Off-Page SEO

## Goal

Build defensible authority through distribution, partnerships, creator submissions, and community visibility. Do not rely on spam links or manipulative link schemes.

## Channels

- Reddit community participation where self-promotion is allowed.
- X/Twitter announcements for new category hubs.
- Creator and channel owner submission campaigns.
- Partner lists and AI/SaaS affiliate collaborations.
- Editorial assets worth citing, such as trend reports and category rankings.

## Link Acquisition Rules

- No paid spam placements.
- No automated forum posting.
- No cloaked redirects.
- No fake profiles or impersonation.
- Prefer branded, transparent outreach.
- Track every campaign source and landing page.

## Assets To Produce

- Monthly Telegram trend reports.
- Top AI bot roundups.
- Category leaderboards.
- Creator submission badges.
- Public methodology page explaining ranking and moderation.

## Metrics

- Referring domains.
- Referral sessions.
- Assisted conversions.
- Branded search growth.
- Owner submissions.
- Link quality and topical relevance.
